### Critical Technical Issues

#### Issue 1: Hearing Test URL Cannibalization
- `/hearing-test` (no trailing slash): 13 impressions, pos 76.6
- `/hearing-test/` (with slash): 7 impressions, pos 74.9
- Google is crawling **two separate URLs** for the same content, splitting PageRank and impressions. This is costing ranking points directly. Fix: canonical tag or 301 redirect one to the other.

#### Issue 2: Six Pages Have Zero Impressions
`/headphone-test/`, `/audio-spectrum-analyzer/`, `/voice-recorder/`, `/mic-quality-test/`, `/bpm-detector/`, `/key-detector/` are invisible to Google. Either not indexed or not internally linked well enough to get crawled. Check: submit to GSC, add to sitemap, add internal links from existing indexed pages.

#### Issue 3: Homepage Has No Keyword Focus
Homepage gets 24 impressions at avg pos 60 for generic terms. It is competing with nothing in particular. A homepage targeting "online audio test" or "audio test online" with proper H1/meta is a low-effort improvement.

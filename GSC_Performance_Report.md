## 6. Priority Actions

1. **Fix /mic-test/** — 239 impressions at position 66 means Google is indexing it but not trusting it. Improve the page's content depth, heading structure, and title tag targeting "online mic test" or "microphone test online."

2. **Push /airpods-latency-test/ to page 1** — already at position 9 with a proven 17–33% CTR on AirPods queries. A few more internal links or content additions could move it into the top 5.

3. **Improve title/meta for /latency-test/** — it handles "sound latency test" (46 impressions) and "audio latency test" (42 impressions) at positions 52–56. These terms are one ranking jump away from generating significant clicks.

4. **Target "latency test bluetooth"** — position 11, likely on the edge of page 1. The /bluetooth-latency-test/ page probably needs minor on-page SEO to get this over the line.

5. **Improve CTR for US/UK/CA/AU traffic** — the site is being shown widely in English-speaking markets but not converting. Better meta descriptions with a clear value proposition ("test your audio latency in seconds — no download required") could lift CTR without changing rankings.
